# Changelog

## Edicts Update

Features:

- Complete rework of the Passives Tree.
- Added new type of item: Rune. Runes can be socketed in Passives Tree in Town, from Ascend panel, changing the effects of the node.
- Added new type of item: Edict. Edicts can be applied to a Grind on startup, increasing monster power and rewards.
- Added a new Evade mechanic: Player and Enemies can get Evade Chance (generic or against specific Damage Type), giving a chance to avoid Damage over Time.

Content:

- Added new magic affixes: added evade chance (boots & cloaks).
- Added new magic affixes: massive increase in Spell Damage at the cost of more Mana (amulets, cloaks & rings).
- Added new magic affixes: massive increase in Attack Damage at the cost of Attack Speed (amulets, cloaks & rings).
- Added tier 7 base items.
- Added Skill: Blizzard.

Balance Changes:

- Increased max player level to 70.
- Decreased cost of Starting Level benediction in Temple.
- Curses & Blessings are now a specific Skill Type instead of being Spells.

Fixes:

- Fix some input fields not getting updated after manually changing value (like in Market).
- Minor visual improvements to the interface.
